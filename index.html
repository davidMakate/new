    An inspired approach to quality Products & Services.

    GCW Bricks & Pavers is a Zimbabwean owned company which Specializes in the Manufacturing of Construction Materials, Construction Aggregates and Providing Construction Services.

    Compressed Cement Bricks , Industrial & Domestic Pavers, Window Sills, Air vents, Driveway Paving and Dura wall Construction are but a few of the Products and Services we offer. Our Vision is to be the Most Trusted supplier of Construction materials and Construction services in Zimbabwe.
